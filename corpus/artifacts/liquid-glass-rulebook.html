Liquid Glass Rulebook

The design-law half of the baseline — distilled from a full audit of the Apple Liquid Glass corpus (HIG, WWDC 219/310/356, materials + component docs). The calibration sheet holds the numbers; this holds the rules. Together they specify the theme's behaviour, not just its look. Rules are paraphrased in our own words.

load-bearing shapes how the package is built — get these wrong and it stops reading as Tahoe

The one meta-rule (APPLE_GENIUS): authentic Apple always wins — mirror it pixel-for-pixel, and where there's no Apple element to mirror, don't invent one. Restraint is the whole aesthetic: over-applied glass, extra tint, and busy motion are the most common failures. Glass is a finish, never a crutch — strip it away and every surface must still be fully legible and operable.
